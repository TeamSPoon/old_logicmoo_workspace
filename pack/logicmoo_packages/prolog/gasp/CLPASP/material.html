Material for readers of ICLP07 paper

  • Complete proofs and concrete syntax of the B-MV-FD language
  • Translator from B to ASP
  • Interpreter of the B language, and some examples:
    • The three barrels
    • A 3 x 3 puzzle
    • A Blocks Problem
  • Interpreter of the B-MV-FD language, and some examples:
    • The three barrels
    • A 3 x 3 puzzle
    • The Protein Folding Problem
    • A better world
  • To use the interpreters, consult/compile them in SICStus Prolog together with the desired action theory. Then call the goal main(N), where N is the length of the plan + 1 (i.e., the number of states involved in the plan).

To Validate/Verify



 
 
 
 

In every editing window, there is a verify button for verifying syntax. Beside, there are also a function for global verification. Click on the button "Global Check" on the main toolbar (Figure 1).

Generally, before use any tools under the "Tool" menu, it's recommended to do a global check.
